The Solaris Legacy

Solaris Terrace was born from a singular vision: to reconcile the quiet intimacy of a family-run estate with the uncompromising comfort of world-class hospitality. Here, we believe that true luxury lies in the harmony of our land, our heritage, and the pursuit of extraordinary peace.

A Sanctuary of Bespoke Elegance

Nestled above the sun-kissed cliffs of the Amalfi Coast, our boutique hotel offers a curated collection of just twelve private suites. We specialize in personalized hospitality, farm-to-table dining, and eco-conscious luxury that leaves a lasting impression on your soul, preserving the beauty of our historic coastline.

Common Enquiries

Everything you need to know before your arrival.

Do you accommodate large private events? expand_more
Solaris Terrace is available for exclusive buyouts for weddings and private celebrations. With only 12 suites, we provide an intimate environment for up to 24 guests, with additional nearby arrangements available through our concierge.
What is your cancellation policy? expand_more
As a boutique property, we offer flexible cancellation up to 30 days prior to arrival for seasonal bookings. Exclusive buyouts and holiday periods may have specific terms provided at the time of reservation.
How do you practice sustainability? expand_more
Sustainability is deeply rooted in our family's values. We use 100% renewable energy, maintain an organic kitchen garden that supplies 80% of our produce, and operate a zero-single-use-plastic estate.
Do you provide airport transfers? expand_more
Yes, our Private Services include luxury transfers from Naples International Airport and Salerno Train Station. We can also arrange private water taxi arrivals directly to our pier.
