Common Enquiries

Everything you need to know before you set sail.

How do I secure a membership? expand_more
Membership at LUXE VOYAGE is by invitation or application. We maintain a curated community to ensure the highest level of service and availability for our properties. Applications are typically processed within 7-10 business days.
What is your cancellation policy? expand_more
We offer flexible cancellation terms for most bookings up to 14 days prior to departure. Private jet charters and exclusive villa buyouts may have specific terms outlined during the booking process.
Do you offer sustainable travel options? expand_more
Sustainability is at our core. Every LUXE VOYAGE booking includes carbon offsetting for travel, and we prioritize properties with Gold-standard environmental certifications and local community integration projects.
Can I book multi-destination itineraries? expand_more
Absolutely. Our concierge team specializes in "Grand Tours" that seamlessly connect multiple destinations with private transfers, ensuring your transition from a Swiss chalet to a Mediterranean yacht is effortless.
